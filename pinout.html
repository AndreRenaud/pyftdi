[1](1, 2) FTDI pins are either configured as input or output. As I2C SDA line is bi-directional, two FTDI pins are required to provide the SDA feature, and they should be connected together and to the SDA I2C bus line. Pull-up resistors on SCK and SDA lines should be used.
[2]FT232H does not support a secondary MPSSE port, only FT2232H and FT4232H do. Note that FT4232H has 4 serial ports, but only the first two interfaces are MPSSE-capable.
